Story 01

The Road That Changed Me

Some roads are simple paths between two places. Others divide life into before and after. The road my family took to Chandrauta was meant to lead us toward safety and opportunity, but life had its own plans. Even now, when I think back, I feel how deeply place and memory can shape a person.

Story 02

What Silence Taught Me

There were years when I said less than I felt. But silence also became a teacher. It taught me observation, patience, and the ability to carry storms without showing them to the world. In that quiet, I learned how inner strength is built.
